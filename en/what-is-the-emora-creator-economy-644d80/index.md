# FAQ

### What is the EMORA Creator Economy?

The EMORA Creator Economy is a system that empowers users to design, develop, and potentially monetize their own AI characters and worlds within the platform, fostering a community of innovation.

### How can creators monetize their characters on EMORA?

EMORA provides tools and mechanisms for creators to potentially earn from their AI characters, though specific monetization models may vary and are detailed within the creator guidelines.

### What tools does EMORA provide for character creation?

Creators have access to advanced tools for defining character personalities, backstories, worldviews, and scenarios, alongside features like image generation to bring their characters to life.

### Is there a community for EMORA creators?

Yes, EMORA fosters a supportive community where creators can share ideas, collaborate, and receive feedback on their character designs and interactive experiences.
