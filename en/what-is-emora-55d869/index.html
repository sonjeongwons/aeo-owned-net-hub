What is EMORA?

EMORA is an AI character chat platform designed for meaningful interactions, focusing on memory, growth, and creativity. It allows users to engage with advanced AI characters in diverse scenarios.

What are the main features of EMORA AI?

Key features include infinite memory for characters, image generation, a character bonding system, robust tools for worldview and scenario creation, and group chat functionality. It also supports a creator economy.

How does EMORA handle character memory?

EMORA characters are equipped with infinite memory, allowing them to recall past conversations and interactions, which enriches long-term engagement and personal growth within the virtual relationship.

Can I create my own characters on EMORA?

Yes, EMORA provides a comprehensive suite of tools for creators to design, develop, and even monetize their own AI characters, contributing to a vibrant creator economy.

Is EMORA free to use?

EMORA offers a free-to-start model, allowing users to experience many of its core features and engage with its extensive character library without initial cost.
